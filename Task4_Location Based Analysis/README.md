# Task 4: Location Based Restaurant Analysis

## Objective
To analyze restaurant distribution, cuisine popularity, and pricing patterns across different city localities.

## Dataset
Zomato restaurant dataset containing locality, city, rating, cost, and cuisine information.

## Steps Performed
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Grouping restaurants by city/locality
- Analysis of restaurant concentration by city
- Calculation of average ratings and costs by location
- Identification of popular cuisines in each area
- Visualization of restaurant distribution

## Key Insights
- Central and commercial areas have higher restaurant density.
- North Indian and Chinese cuisines dominate most areas.
- South Indian cuisine is more popular in traditional residential localities.
- Premium areas show higher average dining costs.
